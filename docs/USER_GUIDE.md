## User Roles

Dayflow HRMS has three main user roles:

### 1. Employee
**Permissions:**
- View personal dashboard
- Clock in/out for attendance
- Submit leave requests
- View own attendance records
- View own payroll information
- Update personal profile

### 2. HR Manager
**Permissions:**
- All Employee permissions, plus:
- View all employee data
- Approve/reject leave requests
- Manage attendance records
- Access payroll reports
- View employee directory

### 3. Admin
**Permissions:**
- All HR Manager permissions, plus:
- Create/edit/delete users
- Manage departments
- Configure system settings
- Generate reports
- Full system access

## Authentication

### First-Time Login

If you're a new employee, you'll receive:
- Employee Code (e.g., EMP001)
- Initial login credentials via email

**Steps:**
1. Open the Dayflow login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Enter your password
4. Click "Sign In"
5. You'll be prompted to change your password on first login

### Sign In

1. Go to the login page
2. Enter your **Email**
3. Enter your **Password**
4. Click **"Sign In"**

### Forgot Password

1. Click **"Forgot Password?"** on the login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Click **"Send Reset Link"**
4. Check your email for the password reset link
5. Click the link and enter your new password
6. Confirm the new password
7. Click **"Reset Password"**

### Change Password

1. Log in to your account
2. Go to **Profile** → **Settings**
3. Click **"Change Password"**
4. Enter current password
5. Enter new password
6. Confirm new password
7. Click **"Update Password"**

## Employee Features

### 1. Clock In/Out

**To Clock In:**
1. Navigate to **Attendance** page
2. Click **"Clock In"** button
3. Confirm your location (optional)
4. Add notes if needed (optional)
5. Click **"Confirm Clock In"**
6. You'll see confirmation with timestamp

**To Clock Out:**
1. Navigate to **Attendance** page
2. Click **"Clock Out"** button
3. Add notes about your day (optional)
4. Click **"Confirm Clock Out"**
5. You'll see total working hours

**Best Practices:**
- Clock in when you start work
- Clock out when you finish
- Add notes for remote work locations
- Contact HR if you forget to clock in/out

### 2. View Attendance History

1. Go to **Attendance** page
2. Select date range or month
3. View your attendance records in table format

**Information Displayed:**
- Date
- Clock In Time
- Clock Out Time
- Working Hours
- Status (Present/Absent/Half Day)
- Notes

**Export Options:**
- Download as PDF
- Export to Excel

### 3. Submit Leave Request

**Steps:**
1. Navigate to **Leave** page
2. Click **"New Leave Request"**
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Leave Type**: Select from dropdown (Sick Leave, Casual Leave, etc.)
   - **Start Date**: Choose start date
   - **End Date**: Choose end date
   - **Reason**: Explain your leave reason
   - **Attach Document**: Upload medical certificate if applicable
4. Review your leave balance
5. Click **"Submit Request"**

**Leave Types:**
- **Sick Leave**: For illness or medical appointments
- **Casual Leave**: Personal reasons
- **Earned Leave**: Vacation time
- **Maternity/Paternity Leave**: For parents
- **Unpaid Leave**: Without salary

**Leave Balance:**
View your remaining leave days for each type in the Leave section.

### 4. Track Leave Requests

1. Go to **Leave** page
2. View all your requests in the table

**Request Status:**
- **Pending**: Awaiting approval
- **Approved**: Leave granted
- **Rejected**: Leave denied (see reason)

**Actions:**
- **View Details**: See full request information
- **Cancel**: Cancel pending request
- **Download**: Get approval letter (for approved leaves)

### 5. View Payroll Information

1. Navigate to **Payroll** page
2. Select month and year
3. View your payslip

**Payslip Details:**
- Basic Salary
- Allowances (HRA, TA, DA, etc.)
- Bonuses
- Deductions (Tax, PF, Insurance)
- Net Salary (Take-home)
- Payment Date
- Payment Status

**Actions:**
- Download payslip as PDF
- Print payslip

### 6. Update Profile

1. Go to **Profile** page
2. Click **"Edit Profile"**
3. Update your information:
   - Personal details (Phone, Address)
   - Emergency contact
   - Bank details (for payroll)
4. Click **"Save Changes"**

**Editable Fields:**
- Phone Number
- Address
- Emergency Contact Name
- Emergency Contact Number
- Bank Account Details

**Non-Editable Fields** (Contact HR to update):
- Name
- Date of Birth
- Employee Code
- Department
- Designation
- Date of Joining

## Attendance Management

### Understanding Attendance Status

**Present**: Clocked in and out for the day
**Absent**: No clock in record
**Half Day**: Less than required working hours
**On Leave**: Approved leave
**Week Off**: Scheduled day off
**Holiday**: Public/Company holiday

### Working Hours Calculation

- **Standard Working Hours**: 9 hours (9:00 AM - 6:00 PM)
- **Lunch Break**: 1 hour (not counted in working hours)
- **Overtime**: Hours worked beyond standard hours
- **Half Day**: Less than 4.5 hours

### Late Arrival Policy

- **Grace Period**: 15 minutes
- **Late**: Arrival after 9:15 AM
- Multiple late arrivals may affect performance review

### Attendance Regularization

If you forgot to clock in/out:
1. Contact your supervisor
2. Fill regularization request form
3. Submit within 2 days
4. Await approval

## Leave Management

### Leave Policy

**Annual Leave Entitlement:**
- Sick Leave: 10 days
- Casual Leave: 12 days
- Earned Leave: 18 days
- Total: 40 days per year

**Leave Rules:**
- Minimum notice: 3 days for planned leave
- Maximum consecutive leave: 15 days
- Unused leave: Can be carried forward (max 10 days)
- Leave encashment: Available for earned leave

### Leave Request Best Practices

1. **Plan Ahead**: Submit requests early
2. **Medical Leave**: Attach doctor's note
3. **Emergency Leave**: Call supervisor immediately
4. **Check Balance**: Ensure sufficient leave balance
5. **Handover**: Inform team about pending work

### Leave Approval Timeline

- Casual Leave: 24-48 hours
- Earned Leave: 3-5 days
- Medical Leave: 24 hours (with certificate)
- Maternity/Paternity: 7-10 days

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

**1. Can't Log In**
- Check email and password
- Try password reset
- Clear browser cache
- Try different browser
- Contact IT support

**2. Clock In Not Working**
- Check internet connection
- Refresh page
- Clear browser cache
- Try different browser
- Contact supervisor

**3. Leave Request Error**
- Check leave balance
- Verify dates (start must be before end)
- Ensure no overlapping requests
- Contact HR if issue persists

**4. Payslip Not Visible**
- Wait until salary payment date
- Check correct month/year selected
- Contact HR/Accounts team

**5. Profile Update Failed**
- Check internet connection
- Verify all required fields filled
- Ensure valid format (phone, email)
- Try again later

## FAQs

### Authentication

**Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions.

**Q: How often should I change my password?**
A: We recommend changing your password every 3 months.

**Q: Can I use the same password for multiple accounts?**
A: No, for security reasons, avoid using the same password.

### Attendance

**Q: What if I forget to clock in?**
A: Contact your supervisor immediately and submit a regularization request.

**Q: Can I clock in from home?**
A: Yes, for remote work. Add location notes when clocking in.

**Q: What happens if I'm late?**
A: 15-minute grace period is provided. Habitual lateness may affect performance reviews.

**Q: How are working hours calculated?**
A: Time between clock in and clock out, minus lunch break (1 hour).

### Leave

**Q: How do I check my leave balance?**
A: Go to Leave page, balance is displayed at the top.

**Q: Can I cancel an approved leave?**
A: Contact your supervisor. They need to cancel it in the system.

**Q: What if my leave request is rejected?**
A: Check the rejection reason and contact your supervisor for clarification.

**Q: Can I apply for half-day leave?**
A: Yes, select same date for start and end, and specify in notes.

### Payroll

**Q: When will I receive my salary?**
A: Last working day of each month.

**Q: Where can I download my payslip?**
A: Go to Payroll page and select the month.

**Q: How is my tax calculated?**
A: Based on your salary slab and submitted investment declarations.

**Q: Can I change my bank details?**
A: Update in Profile section or contact HR for assistance.

### General

**Q: How do I update my personal information?**
A: Go to Profile → Edit Profile. Some fields require HR approval.

**Q: Who do I contact for system issues?**
A: Email it-support@company.com or call the helpdesk.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures.

**Q: Can I access Dayflow on mobile?**
A: Yes, the web interface is mobile-responsive.
